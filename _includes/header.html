How do I find out what my community needs? How do I figure out who to talk to? How do I make change happen? How do I get started?

A growing number of people who work in tech want to be involved in progressive community change, but have never been part of grassroots initiatives before.

This one-day hands-on workshop shows participants how to figure out what their community actually needs, how to rally and organize volunteers, and how to build relationships with local institutions and like-minded groups.
